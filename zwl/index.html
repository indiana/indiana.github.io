Navigation & Compass

Outside the zone? The app shows precise distance and a dynamic direction to the nearest legal camping zone.

9,000+ Tourist POIs

Interactive Map

Fully rendered offline OpenStreetMap maps with clear visualization of zone borders, your location, and points of interest.

🔥

Fire Danger Status

Current fire danger status (BDL data) and clear rules regarding gas stove usage for the day.

100% Offline-First

Map engine and a local database of polygons allow for seamless operation deep in the forest without cell service.
