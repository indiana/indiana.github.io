Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 15, 2026

Focus Timer is developed and maintained as a free/ad-supported application. This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our Application.

By using Focus Timer, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

Personal Data: Focus Timer does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not require account creation, registration, email addresses, or any personal details to use the Application.

Device Permissions: To function properly, the Application requests the following permissions on your device:

  • Internet: Allowed strictly to retrieve advertisement configurations and display ads via our third-party network provider.
  • Advertising ID: Utilized by Google Play Services to provide targeted advertisements and analytics.
  • Exact Alarms: Utilized to ensure the focus timer rings at the exact millisecond your session ends, even when the device is asleep.
  • Notifications: Used to display notification alerts once a focus session completes.
  • Vibrate: Used to trigger haptic feedback at the end of a focus timer.

2. Third-Party Services

The Application uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Below is the link to the privacy policy of the third-party service provider used by the Application:

  • Google AdMob / Google Play Services

3. Data Retention

Because we do not use external servers to store personal data, we do not retain any of your timer targets or session logs. All active session states and focus targets exist locally on your device and are cleared immediately when the app cache is cleared or when the app is uninstalled.

4. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13 years of age. In the case we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our records.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
